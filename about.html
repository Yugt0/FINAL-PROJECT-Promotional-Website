About MotoRiders

Founded in 2005, MotoRiders began as a small motorcycle repair shop with a dream to build a community of riders who share our passion for the open road. What started as a humble garage has grown into a premier destination for motorcycle enthusiasts across the country.

Our mission is simple: provide high-quality motorcycles, accessories, and services while fostering a culture that celebrates the freedom and adventure of riding. We believe in the transformative power of motorcycling and aim to make it accessible to everyone.

With a team of experienced riders and certified technicians, we've established ourselves as experts in the industry. We don't just sell motorcycles; we provide a complete experience that encompasses education, maintenance, and community engagement.

At MotoRiders, we're committed to exceptional customer service, technical excellence, and building lasting relationships with our customers. Whether you're a seasoned rider or just starting your motorcycle journey, we're here to support you every step of the way.

Our Journey

2005

MotoRiders founded as a small repair shop by motorcycle enthusiasts John and Sarah Miller.

2008

Expanded services to include motorcycle sales with our first major brand partnership.

2012

Opened our flagship store and service center, tripling our capacity.

2015

Launched our annual MotoRiders Rally, bringing together riders from across the country.

2018

Expanded our inventory to include exclusive brands and custom motorcycles.

2022

Established our online store to serve customers nationwide.

Today

Continuing our mission to provide exceptional products and services to motorcycle enthusiasts everywhere.
